Mandeep led a team of close to 200 tax professionals trained on Australian Accounting Tax concepts along with this, Mandeep also led a team of close to 800 professionals supporting Deloitte Australia across different streams such as Consulting. Audit and Advisory.

The practice that Mandeep established and led for the Deloitte helped transform its service delivery process.
